Our Goals

Mission

The mission of SDG 6 is to guarantee that every person, including those in underserved communities, can access clean drinking water and adequate sanitation facilities. It strives to prevent water scarcity, reduce contamination, and manage water resources responsibly. SDG 6 also aims to raise awareness, support local water initiatives, and encourage pollution-free environments through safe waste disposal and sustainable practices.

Vision

The vision of SDG 6 is a world where clean water is accessible to everyone, sanitation systems are safe and dignified, and water ecosystems are healthy and well-protected. It imagines communities free from waterborne diseases, where people use water wisely, and natural resources are preserved. Ultimately, SDG 6 envisions a future where water security, environmental health, and human well-being are achieved for all.

SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

SDG 6 focuses on ensuring universal access to clean, safe, and affordable water, along with proper sanitation and hygiene. It aims to improve water quality, reduce pollution, and protect water ecosystems such as rivers and lakes. The goal encourages responsible water use and stronger water management so that communities can maintain a sustainable water supply for future generations.
