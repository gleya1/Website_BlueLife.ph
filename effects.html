Effects of Water Pollution

Learn how polluted water impacts humans, animals, and the environment.

Harm to Marine Life

Toxic chemicals, plastic waste, and oil spills severely damage marine animals. Fish, turtles, and coral reefs suffer, leading to loss of biodiversity and disrupted ecosystems.

Health Problems for Humans

Polluted water can cause diseases such as diarrhea, cholera, and skin infections. Communities without access to clean water face higher health risks every day.

Damage to Ecosystems

Lakes and rivers lose their natural balance when polluted. Plants die, animals migrate, and important habitats are destroyed, affecting the entire food chain.

Economic Impact

Pollution harms fishing industries and tourism, leading to financial loss for communities who rely on water resources for livelihood and income.

Water Shortage

As more water becomes polluted, less clean water is available for drinking, farming, and daily use. This leads to water scarcity, especially in developing communities.
