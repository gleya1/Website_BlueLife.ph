Helpful Tips for Water Conservation

Simple and easy water-saving tips you can do daily to protect our environment.

Tip 1

Turn off the faucet when not in use.

Tip 2

Reuse water whenever possible.

Tip 3

Report leaks in your home.

Tip 4

Use a pail instead of a shower.

Tip 5

Avoid throwing oil into drains.

Tip 6

Encourage your family to conserve.

Turn Off the Faucet

Always turn off the faucet when brushing teeth, scrubbing dishes, or washing hands.

Reuse Water

Use leftover rinsing water for plants, cleaning floors, or flushing the toilet.

Fix Leaks

Small leaks waste thousands of liters monthly. Report and repair leaks immediately.

Use a Pail When Bathing

You can save up to 40% more water by using a dipper and pail instead of a long shower.

Dispose of Oil Properly

Never pour cooking oil down sinks; it blocks pipes and pollutes water systems.

Encourage the Family

Water conservation starts at home. Teach kids simple habits that make a big difference.
